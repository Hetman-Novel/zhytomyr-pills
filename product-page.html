Viagra Connect is an erectile dysfunction treatment which can be bought without a prescription. Viagra Connect contains Sildenafil 50mg and is designed for men aged 18 and over with erectile dysfunction (ED). Viagra Connect works in 25 minutes and lasts up to 4 hours.

It is a safe and discreet over-the-counter medication that can be bought online without seeing your doctor. Instead, you can be approved to purchase Viagra Connect by completing a confidential questionnaire online. Your answers will be reviewed by one of our expert pharmacists.

ED can be associated with a number of contributing conditions, e.g. hypertension, diabetes mellitus, hypercholesterolaemia or cardiovascular disease. As a result, all men with ED should consult their doctor within 6 months for a clinical review of potential underlying conditions and risk factors associated with ED. Please ensure that you make an appointment to see your GP within 6 months of the date of first receiving Viagra Connect.

Is Viagra Connect right for me?

Studies suggest up to 50% of men worldwide** have experienced ED at some stage in their life, many of which might not feel able to visit their GP about it. As it is a pharmacy medicine (PMED), it can now be purchased in confidence online without a prescription.

What are the possible side effects of Viagra Connect?

You should talk to a Pharmacist or doctor if any of the below becomes a concern:

  • Headaches
  • Hot flushes
  • Feeling sick
  • Dizziness
  • Indigestion
  • A congested or blocked nose
  • Changes to your sight such as blurred vision or blue-tinted vision.

You must STOP TAKING Viagra Connect and seek medical attention IMMEDIATELY if you experience any of the following:

  • Chest pain
  • A persistent and sometimes painful erection lasting longer than 4 hours
  • A sudden decrease or loss of vision
  • An allergic reaction such as wheeziness, difficulty breathing or dizziness, swelling of the eyelids, face, lips or throat
  • Serious skin reaction
  • Seizures or fits
